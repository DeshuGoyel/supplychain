# Privacy Policy
**Effective Date**: January 10, 2024

## 1. Introduction

Supply Chain AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Supply Chain AI Control Assistant service.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
- **Account Information**: Name, email address, job title, company name
- **Contact Information**: Phone numbers, mailing addresses
- **Authentication Information**: Usernames, passwords (encrypted)
- **Payment Information**: Billing addresses, payment method details (processed securely by third parties)

### 2.2 Business Data
- **Supply Chain Data**: Inventory levels, supplier information, purchase orders
- **Operational Data**: Shipping information, logistics data, performance metrics
- **Analytics Data**: Usage patterns, feature utilization, performance statistics

### 2.3 Technical Information
- **Log Data**: IP addresses, browser types, access times, pages viewed
- **Device Information**: Device types, operating systems, unique device identifiers
- **Cookies and Tracking**: Session data, preferences, authentication tokens

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Service Provision
- Provide and maintain our supply chain management services
- Process and manage your supply chain data
- Facilitate communication with suppliers and partners
- Generate analytics and insights

### 3.2 Business Operations
- Account management and customer support
- Billing and payment processing
- Service improvement and development
- Security monitoring and fraud prevention

### 3.3 Legal Compliance
- Comply with applicable laws and regulations
- Respond to legal requests and court orders
- Protect our rights and property
- Ensure user safety and service security

## 4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 We Do Not Sell Personal Information
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

### 4.2 Limited Sharing
We may share information in these circumstances:
- **Service Providers**: With trusted partners who assist in service delivery

- **Consent**: When you explicitly consent to sharing

### 4.3 International Transfers
We may transfer data internationally with appropriate safeguards (Standard Contractual Clauses).

## 5. Data Security

### 5.1 Security Measures
- **Encryption**: Data encrypted at rest and in transit

We will notify you of data breaches as required by applicable law, typically within 72 hours.
