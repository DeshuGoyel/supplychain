# Service Level Agreement (SLA)

_Last updated: {{DATE}}_

This Service Level Agreement ("SLA") describes availability and support commitments for the Supply Chain AI Control Assistant service (the "Service").

## 1. Availability Commitment
We target **99.9% uptime** for the Service, measured monthly (excluding permitted downtime).

### 1.1 Definitions
- **Uptime**: The Service is available and responding to valid requests.
- **Downtime**: The Service is unavailable due to errors within our control.

### 1.2 Exclusions (Permitted Downtime)
Availability excludes:
- Scheduled maintenance with advance notice.
- Issues caused by Customer systems, networks, or third-party integrations outside our control.
- Force majeure events.

## 2. Support Response Times
We provide support with the following targets:
- **Initial response time**: within **24 hours** for standard requests.
- **Critical incidents**: prioritized as soon as practicable.

## 3. Incident Response Process
For service incidents:
1. Triage and severity classification.
2. Mitigation and remediation.
3. Post-incident review (for critical incidents), including root cause and corrective actions.

## 4. Remedies and Service Credits
If monthly uptime falls below 99.9% (excluding permitted downtime), Customer may request service credits as follows:
- **99.0% – 99.89%**: 10% credit of monthly fees
- **95.0% – 98.99%**: 25% credit of monthly fees
- **< 95.0%**: 50% credit of monthly fees

Credits are Customer’s sole and exclusive remedy for SLA breaches.

## 5. Requesting Credits
Customer must request credits within 30 days of the end of the affected month and include reasonable details about the downtime.
