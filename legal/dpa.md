# Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

_Last updated: {{DATE}}_

This Data Processing Agreement ("DPA") applies when we process Personal Data on behalf of a customer ("Customer") in connection with the Supply Chain AI Control Assistant service (the "Service").

## 1. Roles
- Customer is the **Data Controller** (or Processor acting on behalf of a Controller).
- We are the **Data Processor** for Customer Personal Data processed under the Service.

## 2. Scope and Instructions
We will process Personal Data only:
- To provide and secure the Service.
- In accordance with Customer’s documented instructions, including these terms and the Customer’s use/configuration of the Service.

## 3. GDPR Compliance
We will:
- Implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect Personal Data.
- Ensure personnel with access to Personal Data are bound by confidentiality.
- Assist Customer, where applicable, with data subject requests and regulatory inquiries.
- Notify Customer of a Personal Data Breach without undue delay after becoming aware of it.

## 4. Sub-processors
Customer authorizes the use of sub-processors necessary to provide the Service. A current list may include:
- Cloud hosting provider (compute and networking)
- Managed PostgreSQL provider
- Object storage provider (for file uploads)
- Email delivery provider (e.g., SendGrid)
- Payment processor (e.g., Stripe)
- Error tracking/monitoring (e.g., Sentry)

We will impose data protection obligations on sub-processors consistent with this DPA.

## 5. Data Transfers
If Personal Data is transferred outside the EEA/UK/Switzerland, we will rely on appropriate safeguards (e.g., SCCs) as required.

## 6. Security Measures
Measures may include:
- TLS encryption in transit.
- Encryption for sensitive secrets (e.g., 2FA secrets/backup codes).
- Access control and least privilege.
- Logging and monitoring.
- Regular backups for business continuity.

## 7. Deletion and Return
Upon termination and subject to applicable law:
- Customer may request deletion or return of Personal Data.
- We may retain limited data (e.g., audit/security logs) as required for compliance and security.

## 8. Audits
Customer may audit compliance with this DPA through reasonable requests, subject to confidentiality and security controls.

## 9. Contact
DPA inquiries: dpa@yourdomain.com
