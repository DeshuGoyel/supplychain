## 1. Introduction

This Cookies Policy explains how Supply Chain AI Control Assistant ("we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies when you use our SaaS platform. This policy should be read together with our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

## 2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

### 2.1 How We Use Cookies
We use cookies to:
- Ensure the security of our Service
- Remember your preferences and settings
- Analyze how you use our Service
- Provide personalized content and features
- Enable certain functionality and features
- Measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns

## 3. Types of Cookies We Use

### 3.1 Essential Cookies
These cookies are strictly necessary for the Service to function properly:

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `session_token` | Maintains your login session | Session |
| `csrf_token` | Protects against cross-site request forgery | Session |
| `consent_preferences` | Stores your cookie consent choices | 1 year |
| `security_headers` | Maintains security settings | Session |

### 3.2 Functional Cookies
These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization:

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `theme_preferences` | Remembers your theme and display preferences | 1 year |
| `language_settings` | Stores your language preference | 1 year |
| `dashboard_layout` | Saves your dashboard layout preferences | 1 year |
| `recent_activity` | Tracks recently accessed features | 30 days |

### 3.3 Performance Cookies
These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our Service:

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `analytics_session` | Anonymous usage analytics for service improvement | 30 minutes |
| `performance_metrics` | Tracks page load times and performance | 1 day |
| `error_tracking` | Captures error information for debugging | 7 days |
| `feature_usage` | Analyzes feature usage patterns | 90 days |

### 3.4 Marketing Cookies
These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements and measure campaign effectiveness:

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `utm_tracking` | Tracks marketing campaign sources | 30 days |

## 5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

### 5.1 Cookie Consent Banner
When you first visit our Service, you'll see a cookie consent banner that allows you to:
- Accept all cookies
- Reject non-essential cookies
- Customize your preferences by category

### 5.2 Browser Settings
You can also control cookies through your browser settings:

#### 5.2.1 Chrome
1. Click the three dots in the top right corner
2. Select "Settings"
3. Click "Privacy and security"
4. Select "Cookies and other site data"
5. Choose your preferred settings

#### 5.2.2 Firefox
1. Click the three lines in the top right corner
2. Select "Options"
3. Click "Privacy & Security"
4. Under "Cookies and Site Data", choose your settings

#### 5.2.3 Safari
1. Click "Safari" in the top menu
2. Select "Preferences"
3. Click the "Privacy" tab
4. Choose your cookie settings

#### 5.2.4 Edge
1. Click the three dots in the top right corner
2. Select "Settings"
3. Click "Privacy, search, and services"
4. Under "Tracking prevention", choose your settings

### 5.3 Opt-Out Tools
You can opt out of specific tracking technologies:

- **Google Analytics**: [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)
- **Marketing emails**: Unsubscribe links in our emails
- **Advertising cookies**: [Your Online Choices](http://www.youronlinechoices.com/)

## 6. Impact of Disabling Cookies

### 6.1 Essential Functions
Disabling essential cookies will:
- Log you out of your account
- Prevent access to secure areas of the Service
- Reset your preferences and settings each visit
- May cause the Service to malfunction

### 6.2 Functional Features
Disabling functional cookies may:
- Reset your theme and language preferences
- Cause dashboard layouts to revert to defaults
- Lose recent activity tracking

### 6.3 Analytics and Marketing
Disabling performance and marketing cookies will:
- Prevent us from understanding how you use the Service
- Stop personalized content recommendations
- Disable marketing campaign tracking
- Reduce our ability to improve the Service
