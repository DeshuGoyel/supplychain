## 1. Introduction

This Cookies Policy explains how Supply Chain AI ("we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our Supply Chain AI Control Assistant platform. This policy should be read in conjunction with our Privacy Policy.

## 2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

### Types of Cookies We Use

**Essential Cookies**
- Purpose: Required for the website to function properly
- Duration: Session or short-term (24 hours)
- Examples: Authentication tokens, security tokens, load balancing

**Functional Cookies**
- Purpose: Remember your preferences and settings
- Duration: Up to 12 months
- Examples: Language preferences, theme settings, UI customizations

**Analytics Cookies**
- Purpose: Help us understand how visitors use our website
- Duration: Up to 24 months
- Examples: Page views, session duration, click patterns

**Marketing Cookies**
- Purpose: Deliver relevant advertisements and track campaign effectiveness
- Duration: Up to 12 months
- Examples: Remarketing pixels, conversion tracking

## 6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

### 6.1 Browser Settings
You can control cookies through your browser settings:

**Google Chrome:**
1. Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
2. Choose your preferred cookie settings

**Mozilla Firefox:**
1. Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
2. Configure your preferences

**Safari:**
1. Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
2. Select your options

**Microsoft Edge:**
1. Settings > Site permissions > Cookies and site data
2. Manage your preferences

### 6.2 Opt-Out Tools
You can opt out of specific tracking:

**Google Analytics:** https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
**Google Ads:** https://adssettings.google.com/authenticated
**Facebook:** https://www.facebook.com/settings?tab=ads
**LinkedIn:** https://www.linkedin.com/psettings/member-data-and-advertising

### 6.3 Our Cookie Settings
You can manage your cookie preferences directly on our website:

- **Essential Cookies:** Always active (cannot be disabled)
- **Functional Cookies:** Can be enabled/disabled
- **Analytics Cookies:** Can be enabled/disabled
- **Marketing Cookies:** Can be enabled/disabled

## 7. Impact of Disabling Cookies

### 7.1 Essential Cookies
If you disable essential cookies:
- You will not be able to log in to your account
- Security features may not work properly
- Core website functionality may be unavailable

### 7.2 Functional Cookies
If you disable functional cookies:
- Your preferences won't be saved between visits
- You'll need to reconfigure settings each time
- Some features may not work as expected

### 7.3 Analytics Cookies
If you disable analytics cookies:
- We won't be able to improve our services based on usage data
- You won't be included in analytics reports
- Some features that rely on analytics may not work

### 7.4 Marketing Cookies
If you disable marketing cookies:
- You may see less relevant advertisements
- Some personalized features won't work
- Campaign effectiveness won't be measured
