# Acceptable Use Policy

**Effective Date:** January 12, 2025  
**Last Updated:** January 12, 2025

## 1. Purpose and Scope

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") establishes guidelines for the appropriate use of the Supply Chain AI Control Assistant platform. This policy applies to all users, administrators, and third parties who access or use our services.

## 2. General Principles

Users of our platform must:
- Comply with all applicable laws and regulations
- Respect the rights and privacy of other users
- Use the service in good faith for legitimate business purposes
- Maintain the security and integrity of the platform
- Respect intellectual property rights
- Act responsibly and professionally

## 3. Prohibited Uses

### 3.1 Illegal Activities
You may not use our platform to:
- Engage in or promote illegal activities under any applicable law
- Violate export control laws or sanctions
- Engage in money laundering, fraud, or financial crimes
- Support or promote terrorism or other violent activities
- Violate intellectual property laws or regulations

### 3.2 Security Violations
You may not:
- Attempt to gain unauthorized access to our systems or other users' accounts
- Perform penetration testing or vulnerability scanning without authorization
- Introduce malware, viruses, or other malicious code
- Attempt to bypass security controls or authentication mechanisms
- Share or distribute tools designed for unauthorized access
- Engage in network sniffing or packet capture on our infrastructure

### 3.3 Abuse and Harassment
You may not:
- Harass, threaten, or intimidate other users or our personnel
- Engage in hate speech or discriminatory conduct
- Impersonate other individuals or entities
- Spread misinformation that could cause harm
- Engage in stalking or other invasive behaviors

### 3.4 Spam and Unwanted Communications
You may not:
- Send unsolicited bulk communications to users
- Use our platform to send spam or junk mail
- Engage in phishing or social engineering attacks
- Send messages that could be considered harassment
- Post or transmit unwanted promotional content

### 3.5 Content Violations
You may not:
- Upload or share content that is illegal, harmful, or offensive
- Infringe on intellectual property rights of others
- Share confidential or proprietary information without authorization
- Post false or misleading information
- Engage in deceptive practices

## 4. Business-Specific Restrictions

### 4.1 Supply Chain Data Integrity
Users must:
- Provide accurate and truthful supply chain data
- Not manipulate data to gain unfair business advantages
- Maintain proper authorization for data access and modification
- Report data integrity issues promptly
- Comply with industry standards and regulations

### 4.2 Platform Usage Limits
Users may not:
- Exceed reasonable usage limits or quotas
- Use automated systems to circumvent rate limits
- Overload our systems with excessive requests
- Share accounts or credentials with unauthorized parties
- Use the platform for commercial purposes outside approved agreements

## 5. Data Protection and Privacy

### 5.1 Personal Data Handling
Users must:
- Process personal data only with appropriate legal basis
- Obtain necessary consents for data processing
- Implement appropriate data protection measures
- Respect individuals' data protection rights
- Report data breaches promptly

### 5.2 Business Data Security
Users must:
- Protect confidential business information
- Implement access controls for sensitive data
- Follow data retention and disposal policies
- Ensure data accuracy and completeness
- Comply with industry-specific regulations

## 6. Intellectual Property Rights

### 6.1 Respect for IP Rights
Users may not:
- Infringe on copyrights, trademarks, or patents
- Use our platform to distribute pirated content
- Copy our proprietary software or documentation
- Reverse engineer or decompile our systems
- Use our branding or trademarks without permission

### 6.2 User-Generated Content
Users retain rights to their content but grant us a license to:
- Process and store their content as necessary for service provision
- Display content within the platform
- Generate analytics and insights from content usage
- Backup and archive content for service continuity

## 7. Third-Party Integrations

### 7.1 External Systems
Users must ensure that:
- Integration with external systems complies with our terms
- Third-party services meet security and privacy standards
- Data sharing with third parties is properly authorized
- External service providers agree to appropriate data protection terms

### 7.2 API Usage
Users accessing our APIs must:
- Use APIs only for authorized purposes
- Respect rate limits and usage quotas
- Implement proper error handling and retry logic
- Protect API keys and credentials
- Not share API access without authorization

## 8. Monitoring and Enforcement

### 8.1 Monitoring Activities
We monitor platform usage to:
- Ensure compliance with this AUP
- Detect and prevent security threats
- Protect system integrity and availability
- Investigate suspected violations
- Improve service quality and performance

### 8.2 Investigation Procedures
When investigating potential violations, we may:
- Review account activity and usage patterns
- Examine user communications and content
- Cooperate with law enforcement when required
- Seek technical evidence of violations
- Consult with affected users or third parties

## 9. Violation Consequences

### 9.1 Warning System
For minor violations, we may:
- Issue warnings and educational guidance
- Provide opportunities for corrective action
- Implement temporary usage restrictions
- Require additional verification or authentication

### 9.2 Enforcement Actions
For serious or repeated violations, we may:
- Suspend or terminate user accounts
- Restrict access to certain features or services
- Report violations to relevant authorities
- Pursue legal remedies for damages
- Share information with other service providers

### 9.3 Appeal Process
Users may appeal enforcement actions by:
- Submitting written appeals within 30 days
- Providing evidence of corrective actions
- Demonstrating compliance with this AUP
- Requesting review by senior management
- Pursuing alternative dispute resolution

## 10. Reporting Violations

### 10.1 How to Report
To report violations of this AUP:
- Email: abuse@supplychainai.com
- Include detailed description of the violation
- Provide supporting evidence or documentation
- Specify the affected user or system components
- Include contact information for follow-up

### 10.2 Response Timeline
We will:
- Acknowledge reports within 24 hours
- Investigate reported violations promptly
- Provide status updates for ongoing investigations
- Share outcomes when legally permitted
- Implement appropriate corrective measures

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this AUP to:
- Reflect changes in legal requirements
- Address new types of violations or threats
- Improve clarity or user understanding
- Align with industry best practices
- Support business model changes

Changes will be communicated through:
- Email notifications to affected users
- In-app notifications and alerts
- Updates to our website documentation
- Advance notice for material changes
- User acknowledgment requirements
