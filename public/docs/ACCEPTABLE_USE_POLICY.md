# Acceptable Use Policy

**Effective Date:** [Date]  
**Last Updated:** [Date]

## 1. Introduction

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") outlines the acceptable use of Supply Chain AI Control Assistant ("Service"). By using our Service, you agree to comply with this policy. Violations may result in account suspension or termination.

## 2. General Principles

### 2.1 Lawful Use
You must use the Service only for lawful purposes and in accordance with all applicable laws, regulations, and third-party agreements.

### 2.2 Respect for Others
You must not use the Service in a way that harms, threatens, or interferes with other users, our operations, or the Service itself.

### 2.3 Security and Integrity
You must not attempt to compromise the security or integrity of the Service or other users' data.

## 3. Prohibited Activities

### 3.1 Illegal Activities
You may not use the Service for any illegal activities, including but not limited to:
- Fraud, money laundering, or other financial crimes
- Copyright infringement or intellectual property theft
- Distribution of malware or viruses
- Cyberstalking or harassment
- Gambling or betting operations
- Drug trafficking or other illegal trade

### 3.2 Security Violations
You may not:
- Attempt to gain unauthorized access to our systems or other users' accounts
- Use automated tools to scan for vulnerabilities without authorization
- Distribute denial-of-service attacks
- Upload or transmit viruses, malware, or malicious code
- Attempt to reverse engineer, decompile, or disassemble the Service
- Use the Service to probe, scan, or test the vulnerability of any system

### 3.3 Data Privacy Violations
You may not:
- Upload, store, or process personal data without proper legal basis
- Violate data protection laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
- Process sensitive personal data without adequate security measures
- Share or disclose other users' information without consent
- Use the Service to collect personal data about others without permission

### 3.4 Spam and Abuse
You may not:
- Send unsolicited commercial communications ("spam")
- Engage in phishing or social engineering attacks
- Create fake accounts or impersonate other individuals
- Use automated means to create accounts or submit data
- Engage in click fraud or other manipulation of metrics

### 3.5 Content Restrictions
You may not upload, store, or share content that:
- Is defamatory, threatening, or harassing
- Contains hate speech or discriminatory content
- Is sexually explicit or pornographic
- Contains violent or graphic content
- Infringes on intellectual property rights
- Is misleading, deceptive, or fraudulent

### 3.6 Resource Abuse
You may not:
- Use the Service to mine cryptocurrency
- Run computationally intensive processes that degrade performance
- Exceed rate limits or usage quotas
- Use the Service for file storage beyond allowed limits
- Engage in activities that could cause service degradation

## 4. Supply Chain Data Integrity

### 4.1 Accurate Information
You must provide accurate and truthful information when using supply chain management features.

### 4.2 Data Quality
You are responsible for maintaining the quality and accuracy of your supply chain data.

### 4.3 Regulatory Compliance
Your use of the Service must comply with relevant supply chain regulations and industry standards.

## 5. Account Management

### 5.1 Account Security
- You must maintain strong passwords and enable 2FA when available
- You are responsible for all activities under your account
- You must immediately report unauthorized access or security breaches

### 5.2 Account Sharing
- Each account is intended for use by a single organization
- Account credentials may not be shared outside your organization
- You may not resell or redistribute access to the Service

### 5.3 Billing and Payments
- You must provide accurate billing information
- You are responsible for all charges under your account
- You may not use false payment information or engage in payment fraud

## 6. White-Label and Branding

### 6.1 Appropriate Branding
White-label customers may only use branding that:
- Does not infringe on intellectual property rights
- Complies with applicable laws and regulations
- Does not mislead users about the nature of the service

### 6.2 Content Standards
Custom branding and content must:
- Be appropriate for business use
- Comply with our community standards
- Not contain prohibited content as outlined in Section 3.5

## 7. Third-Party Integrations

### 7.1 SSO Integration
- You must only integrate with legitimate identity providers
- SSO configurations must comply with your organization's security policies
- You are responsible for maintaining secure SSO integration

### 7.2 API Usage
- API usage must comply with documented rate limits and usage patterns
- You may not use the API to replicate our service for competitive purposes
- API keys must be kept secure and not shared

## 8. Monitoring and Enforcement

### 8.1 Monitoring
We may monitor your use of the Service to:
- Ensure compliance with this policy
- Protect the security and integrity of the Service
- Investigate suspected violations
- Improve service quality and performance

### 8.2 Investigation
When investigating potential violations, we may:
- Review account activity and data usage
- Contact users for clarification
- Request additional information or documentation
- Suspend accounts pending investigation

### 8.3 Enforcement Actions
Violations may result in:
- Written warnings
- Temporary account suspension
- Permanent account termination
- Legal action where appropriate
- Reporting to law enforcement for illegal activities

## 9. Reporting Violations

### 9.1 How to Report
Report suspected violations to: abuse@supplychainai.com

Include:
- Description of the violation
- Account or user information involved
- Evidence or supporting documentation
- Your contact information for follow-up

### 9.2 Anonymous Reporting
Anonymous reports are accepted, though follow-up may be limited without contact information.

## 10. Appeals Process

### 10.1 Appeal Submission
If you believe your account was suspended or terminated in error, you may appeal by contacting: appeals@supplychainai.com

### 10.2 Appeal Review
Appeals will be reviewed within 5 business days. Decisions on appeals are final.

- In-Service notifications
- Updates to our website

### 11.2 Effective Date
Changes take effect 30 days after notification unless you terminate your account earlier.
